Community

There are various committees in our College, such as Gymkhana Committee,Anti-Ragging Committee, Staff Committee etc.

You Should Know.....

1.Gymkhana Committee: In Gymkhana Committee there are various posts such as Gymkhana Secretary(GS), Cultural Secretary, Anual Gathering Secretary, Sports Secretary etc. The motive of establishing such committee in college was for encouraging students for comming together and perform various events in college.

2.Anti-Ragging Committee: In this committee there are Teachers as well as Students. This committee is responsible for all ragging related issues. The main motive for establishing this committee, is that no one should involve in the ragging related activities in the college campus.
